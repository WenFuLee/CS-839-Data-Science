The harsh reality of one kid s life as a criminal on the inner city streets is the focus of this film from director Jose Novoa Searching for an escape from the lowest rungs of society , young Jairo falls in with a local drug ring , working as a hitman Despite the lucrative aspects of the business , Jairo eventually wants out of the world of crime Unfortunately , his boss doesn t take kindly to that decision ~ Matthew Tobey , Rovi
